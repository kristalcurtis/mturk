Guess how many dots are in an image

  • In this HIT, you will be shown an image which will consist of many dots. Your task is to accurately and quickly guess the number of dots in the image.

  • We will record your guess and the time you spend on the task (between starting and ending the guess).

  • After you provide your guess, we will ask you to rate your performance on the task with respect to both speed and accuracy. We will also ask you to provide your demographic information.

  • The timer will begin, and the image will be shown, once you press the button labeled "Start guessing."

  • Please complete the entire HIT to receive payment.
